Thirdly; "u", "del" and "mark"

Fourthly; "sub" and "sup"

-sub command;
Subscript text is typically used for mathematical and chemical formulas.

->H2O, is the chemical formula for water.
->Log10=1

-sup command;
Its usually used for mathematical exponents

->x2+y2
->23=8
